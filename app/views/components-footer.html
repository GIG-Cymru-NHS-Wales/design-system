Footer

Use the footer to show users they are on an NHS service and to help them find links they expect at the bottom of our pages.

NHS Wales default footer

NHS Wales websites have the default footer with links to policies and other meta information about your service.

Keep links to a minimum, especially in a transactional journey. Too many links can distract users. Link to specific policies for the transaction, if appropriate. If your total number of links is less than 7, arrange them horizontally in lines.



Open example in a new tab


NHS Wales footer with navigation links

If your users need lots of footer links to help them navigate a complex information architecture, include navigation links and arrange the links in columns.
