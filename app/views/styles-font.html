Our fonts and typographic styles, and how to apply them.

Frutiger

Frutiger is the brand font for the NHS. NHS Wales has a licence for 3 weights of the font which NHS organisations in Wales can sign up to use.

Use Arial when Frutiger isn't available.

font-family: “Frutiger W01”, Arial, sans-serif;

Visit NHS Design System to find out how to use the font and typographic styles.
