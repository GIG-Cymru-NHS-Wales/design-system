Accessibility statement

We aim to make the NHS digital service manual as accessible and easy to use as possible.

We've designed our pages to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines to level AA - and in some cases AAA - and we've tested them with users with access needs.

How to contact us

If you have any questions about accessibility, please email us at design-system@wales.nhs.uk. We will respond within 5 working days.
