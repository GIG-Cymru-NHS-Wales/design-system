Patterns

Patterns are tested and agreed solutions to common needs. You can reuse them across NHS services to make them more consistent.

We have 3 types of patterns:

  • service patterns to allow users iteract with a service, for example, manage a suscription
  • task patterns to help users complete a task, for example, give us different kinds of information
  • page patterns to improve navigation

Patterns often use more than 1 component and the guidance explains how to adapt them to the context.
