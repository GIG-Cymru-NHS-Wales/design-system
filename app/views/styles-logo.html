Logo

Our Logo and how to apply it.

NHS Wales has its own brand, which differs from that of NHS England.

Our brand has different logos in both English and Welsh simultaneously as part of Welsh government regulations.

The NHS Wales Logo Guidelines dictate how the logo must appear on digital platforms, including websites, applications, intranets, and extranets.

NHS Wales logos are displayed in white on blue headers.

No logo is required in footers.
